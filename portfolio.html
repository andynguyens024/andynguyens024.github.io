Crazy Wealthy AsianZ

This is a story between two star-crossed lovers, Bill and Mirabelle, who begin dating. Mirabelle faces criticism from Bill's family because she was an American. Mirabelle and Bill both make decisions that determine whether they get married or get separated.

During the incremental process of the program, we made our story based off of three decisions. We also determined the settings andthe characters in each scene, and we gradually plotted actions that would ultimately affect the outcomes of the story. During the iterative development process, we returned to the story and added descriptions. A difficulty I faced was accessing the “repl” that my partner worked on; we resolved this by communicating with each other of what times we were available. Another difficulty was adding quotation marks and any special characters to our text which was quickly resolved by finding where our other apostrophes were placed and adding “\”..

Procedural abstraction is introduced by organizing each scene since different parts of the story provides the user a story of what will happen. When separated into functions, the reader has the oppotunity to create his or her own ending and scenes. The advantage is that it allows the reader to see each program.

A story I admire is the story of Cinderella. This story is similar to Crazy Wealthy Asianz. Cinderella is similar to Mirabelle and left. Prince Charming attemptted to find her to return back her slipper, just like Bill. The passion for each other resonates to me as I am still trying to find a wholesome significant other, particularly one that can do chores.
